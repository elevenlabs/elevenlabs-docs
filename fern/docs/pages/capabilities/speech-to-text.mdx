## Overview

ElevenLabs [speech-to-text (STT)](/docs/api-reference/speech-to-text) API turns spoken audio into text with high accuracy. Our Scribe v1 [model](/docs/models) adapts to textual cues across 32 languages and multiple voice styles and can be used to:

- Transcribe podcasts, interviews, and other audio content
- Create voice assistants
- Generate transcripts for meetings and other audio recordings

## High accuracy

The Scribe v1 model is capable of transcribing audio from up to 16 speakers with high accuracy. Optionally it can also transcribe audio events like laughter, applause, and other non-speech sounds.
