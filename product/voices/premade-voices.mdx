Premade voices are a curated set of voices optimized for our core use cases and made available to all ElevenLabs users by default.

## Using Premade Voices

Unlike voices you add from the Voice Library or new <a href="docs/voices/voice-lab/instant-voice-cloning">Instant Voice Clones (IVCs)</a> or <a href="docs/voices/voice-lab/professional-voice-cloning">Professional Voice Clones (PVCs)</a> you create, premade voices are not accessed via your <a href="docs/voices/voice-lab/overview">VoiceLab</a>.

Instead, there are 2 ways to find and use premade voices:

- <b>API</b>: Calls to the /voices endpoint will fetch all premade voices in
  addition to voices added to your VoiceLab. See the <a href="https://elevenlabs.io/docs/api-reference/get-voices">
    voices API documentation
  </a> for more details.{" "}

- <b>UI</b>: Open the voice dropdown menu by clicking the voice name in the
  bottom left-hand corner of the text-to-speech or speech-to-speech screen:
  <img
    width="600"
    height="100%"
    src="/voices/images/voice-select.png"
  />

  You'll find our premade voices under the 'Premade' heading. To hear a sample of the voice, click the circular icon next to the voice name.
