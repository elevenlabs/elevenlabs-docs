To select a voice, click the voice name in the bottom left-hand corner of the text-to-speech or voice changer box:

<img width="400" height="100%" src="/documentation/voices/images/voice-select.png" />

In the dropdown menu, you can select or search for multiple categories of voices:

- Recently used
- Professional (your own Professional Voice Clone, and those you've saved from the Voice Library)
- Cloned (Instant Voice Clones you've created)
- Generated voices created using our <a href="/docs/documentation/voices/voice-lab/voice-design">Voice Design</a> tool
- <a href="/docs/documentation/voices/default-voices">Default voices</a>

<img width="400" height="100%" src="/documentation/voices/images/default-voices.png" />
